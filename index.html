The Crisis

WHEN NETWORKS
FAIL, PEOPLE DIE

In the 2018 California Camp Fire, 17 cell towers burned down — cutting thousands off from 911 services at the exact moment they needed help most.

During the 2023 Maui wildfires, hundreds of 911 calls went unanswered because dispatch systems were completely overwhelmed by simultaneous emergencies.

Civilians trapped in fires have no reliable way to call for help. Firefighters enter buildings with no real-time intelligence on victim locations. The technology gap costs lives — and it's getting worse every year.

The Process

HOW IT WORKS

1
Alarm Triggers
When a fire alarm activates, all R.E.A.C.H. units in the building power on instantly and automatically.
2
AI Detects Voice
Onboard AI listens for human sounds. Trapped civilians are detected even when they cannot speak loudly.
3
Responders Notified
"Human activity detected — 3rd floor stairwell." Real-time alerts reach firefighter radios via APX system.
4
Two-Way Voice
Civilians speak directly with firefighters. APX Noise Suppression ensures clarity through smoke and chaos.
